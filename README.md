# 📊 Strategy Case Study – Consulting Assignment

## 📌 Overview
This repository contains my submission for a **Strategy Intern / Consultant Case Study**.  
The objective was to analyze organizational challenges and propose a structured, actionable strategy to improve growth, profitability, and employee experience in a fast-growing technology consulting firm.

---

## 📁 Repository Contents

- 📊 **Strategy - Excel Assignment.xlsx**  
  Data analysis, transformations, and supporting calculations used for insights.

- 📽️ **Strategy Case Study Presentation.pptx**  
  Final presentation deck outlining the strategic recommendations, roadmap, and execution plan.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
The organization is facing multiple challenges across:

- Training & Upskilling  
- Innovation & Collaboration  
- Employee Engagement  
- Presales & Estimation Accuracy  
- Project Execution & Delivery  
- Account Strategy  
- AI Adoption  
- Resource Planning  
- Business Unit Silos  

---

## 🧭 Strategic Approach

The solution is structured around **5 key strategic pillars**:

1. **People & Capability Excellence**  
2. **Delivery & Operational Excellence**  
3. **AI-Led Transformation**  
4. **Client & Account Strategy**  
5. **Integrated Organization & Collaboration**

---

## 🚀 Key Highlights

- Defined a **12-month transformation roadmap** (Stabilize → Build → Scale)
- Introduced **AI-driven solutions** across:
  - Presales (effort estimation, proposal generation)
  - Delivery (risk prediction, project tracking)
  - Resource planning (forecasting, workload balancing)
- Designed **governance framework** with KPIs and tracking mechanisms
- Identified **quick wins** for immediate impact (first 2 quarters)

---

## 🤖 AI Enablement Focus

Key opportunities identified:
- AI-powered learning & upskilling
- Intelligent project management dashboards
- Predictive analytics for resource allocation
- GenAI-based proposal automation
